Privacy-First Extension

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

At Simple Color Mixer, we believe that your digital workspace should be private and secure. This policy outlines our commitment to your data security and transparency.

1. Data Collection & Storage

Simple Color Mixer is designed as a Zero-Server utility. We do not collect, store on external servers, or transmit any personal information or browsing history.

  • No Personal Data: We do not ask for or access your name, email, or location.
  • No Usage Tracking: We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics.
  • Local Storage: When you use the "Add to Palette" feature, hex codes are saved locally on your device using the chrome.storage.local API. This data is never synced, shared, or uploaded.
  • Local Execution: All color mixing math and UI rendering happen entirely on your machine.

2. Browser Permissions

In accordance with the "Principle of Least Privilege," this extension requires only one essential permission:

  • Storage: Used exclusively to allow you to save and retrieve your custom color palettes locally.

This extension cannot read your website data, access your tabs, or see your browsing history.

3. Third-Party Disclosure

Since we do not collect any data, there is no data to sell, trade, or transfer. The extension does not communicate with any external APIs, advertising networks, or servers.

4. Compliance

This extension complies with the Google Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
